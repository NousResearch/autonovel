## Discrepancies and Gaps

- **Dottie's birth year:** seed.txt states she died "at the age of 75" on March 20, 2024, implying birth in 1948 or 1949 depending on whether her birthday had passed. world.md and characters.md list her dates as "(1949–2024)." The 1949 birth year should be treated as the working canon figure, with awareness that the precise month of birth is unconfirmed.
- **Andy's age:** Described only as "school-age" at the time of the naming. No specific age is given in any source document.
- **Jim and Andrea's ages:** Not confirmed in any source document. Described as "mid-to-late career."
- **The previous facility:** Confirmed to have existed and to have been award-winning. Its name, location, and the reason the Paynes left it are not stated anywhere in the source documents.
- **Andy's exact words:** All three source documents agree on the wording: *"Dad, why don't you call it A Pup's Valley?"* This is treated as confirmed.
- **The seventeen words in the minutes:** All source documents agree on the exact quoted language from the official minutes. This is treated as confirmed.
- **What Andrea said beyond the seventeen words:** The source documents explicitly acknowledge this is not recorded. The novel must earn the right to render it.
- **Councilman Shannon's first name:** Not provided in any source document.
- **The date of Andy's school pickup / the naming:** Not pinned to a specific date in any source document. Placed in the 2018–2019 planning period by context.
